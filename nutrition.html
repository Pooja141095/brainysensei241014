Nutritious Foods for Your Fitness Journey

Proper nutrition is essential for achieving your fitness goals. Here are some nutritious foods to include in your diet:

1. Lean Proteins

Proteins are crucial for muscle repair and growth. Include sources like:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Turkey
  • Fish (Salmon, Tuna)
  • Legumes (Lentils, Chickpeas)

2. Whole Grains

Whole grains provide sustained energy. Consider adding:

  • Brown Rice
  • Quinoa
  • Oats
  • Whole Wheat Bread

3. Fruits and Vegetables

Rich in vitamins and minerals, they support overall health:

  • Broccoli
  • Spinach
  • Blueberries
  • Bananas

4. Healthy Fats

Healthy fats are vital for hormone production and joint health:

  • Avocados
  • Nuts and Seeds
  • Olive Oil
  • Fatty Fish

5. Hydration

Staying hydrated is key to performance and recovery. Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
